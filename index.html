Dr. A.M. Abirami

Professor, Dept. of Information Technology

Thiagarajar College of Engineering, Madurai

Profile

Computer Science Engineer from Bharathiyar University, Coimbatore, with a B.E. degree (1999). Former Software Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. (1999–2006). M.E. from Anna University Tirunelveli (2010). PhD in Text Analytics and Semantic Web from Anna University Chennai (2018).

Passionate about improving teaching-learning methodologies in engineering education. Certified by Cambridge International (Wipro’s Mission10X programme). Recognized supervisor for PhD scholars at Anna University Chennai.
